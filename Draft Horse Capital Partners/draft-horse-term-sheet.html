9. Risk Disclosure
Key Risk Factors
Illiquidity Risk
Investments in the fund are illiquid. Redemption requests subject to fund liquidity, with potential delays or restrictions.
Credit Risk
Fund returns depend on successful debt collection and borrower creditworthiness. Defaults or payment delays may impact distributions.
Sector Risk
Economic downturns, industry disruptions, or regulatory changes may negatively impact portfolio performance across verticals.
Interest Rate Risk
Changes in interest rates may affect the valuation and performance of fixed-income debt instruments held by the fund.
Manager Risk
Fund performance depends on the skill and experience of Draft Horse Wealth Management LLC and its vertically-aligned partners.
No Secondary Market
There is no secondary market for fund membership interests. Investors should plan for long-term commitment.
Leverage Risk
Fund may utilize leverage on certain verticals, which amplifies both returns and risks. Margin calls or forced liquidation may occur.
Valuation Risk
Non-quoted investments may be difficult to value. NAV calculations involve subjective assessments and may not reflect true market value.
