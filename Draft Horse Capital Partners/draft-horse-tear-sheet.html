Overview
Draft Horse Capital Partners is a multi-strategy private debt fund structured to deliver fixed contractual returns to investors with zero management or performance fees. The fund deploys capital across five complementary verticals — aviation, mining and energy commodities, direct e-commerce lending, real estate, and fine art — each managed through a dedicated operating entity with domain-specific expertise and isolated liability.
