Recommended Architecture
1
Investor signs NDA, accesses data room, completes subscription docs via Passthrough. KYC/AML cleared automatically.
2
Investor data flows into Juniper Square. LP position created. Portal access granted. Wire instructions delivered.
3
Monthly: Flow auto-generates distribution notices with individualized wire details. LP confirms and wires. Juniper Square records and updates portal.
