The Platform
Dark Horse Wealth Management
Dark Horse Wealth Management LLC is the institutional investment platform of the Caraway family office. deploying capital into uncorrelated, appreciating asset classes and cash-flowing businesses — engineered for high compression ratios, consistent fixed-rate returns, and investment opportunities backed by tangible, appreciating collateral, the platform deploys capital across four operating verticals through dedicated portfolio companies — each with domain-specific expertise and isolated liability structures.
Investment Thesis
Traditional alternative allocations cluster around the same overexposed asset classes. Dark Horse targets uncorrelated, appreciating asset classes and cash-flowing businesses that institutional portfolios chronically underweight — aviation, natural resources, fine art, real estate, and Private Credit. Each position is selected for three characteristics: the underlying asset must be appreciating, the investment must generate contractual cash flow, and every position must be backed by tangible collateral. This combination creates high compression ratios — outsized fixed-rate yields relative to the risk profile of the underlying investment — aviation, natural resources, real estate, and Private Credit. The platform charges zero management fees and zero performance fees, aligning GP and investor interests entirely through principal capital commitment alongside every limited partner.
