Fund Overview

Draft Horse Capital Partners LLC

Texas LLC · Reg D 506(c) · Section 3(c)(1)
Multi-strategy institutional debt fund offering diversified private credit exposure across six tangible-asset verticals — aviation, mining and metals, art-backed bonds, real estate, e-commerce lending, and custom institutional allocations. Investors receive a fixed rate of return on deployed capital. Designed for sovereign wealth funds, family offices, institutional banks, and accredited investors seeking uncorrelated alternative exposure through a single vehicle with zero fee drag. No management fees. No performance fees. Full principal alignment through GP ownership of underlying assets and significant capital commitment to fund collateral.

Draft Horse Capital Partners LLC
Draft Horse Capital Partners LLC is a Texas limited liability company managed by Draft Horse Wealth Management LLC, operating under Dark Horse Wealth Management LLC. Securities are offered pursuant to Regulation D, Rule 506(c) under the Securities Act of 1933. The fund relies on the exemption from registration under Section 3(c)(1) of the Investment Company Act of 1940 and is limited to no more than 100 beneficial owners. This offering is available exclusively to verified accredited investors. All materials are indicative only and subject to the definitive Private Placement Memorandum. Stated targets are not guaranteed returns. Principal is subject to loss.
