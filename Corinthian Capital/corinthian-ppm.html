Corinthian Capital Partners LLC · Regulation D · Private Placement
Strictly Confidential
This document is provided exclusively to qualified recipients under applicable securities exemptions. Reproduction, distribution, or disclosure without prior written consent is strictly prohibited. This is not an offer to sell or a solicitation of an offer to buy any securities.
Overview
This Private Placement Memorandum sets forth the terms and conditions of the offering of membership interests in Corinthian Capital Fund LLC. The fund offers a fixed contractual return of 2% per month (24% per annum) through private lending.
Offering Terms
Securities are offered pursuant to Regulation D, Rule 506(b) under the Securities Act of 1933. No general solicitation or advertising is permitted. All investors must have a pre-existing substantive relationship with the issuer. Up to 35 sophisticated non-accredited investors may participate.
Use of Proceeds
Proceeds from the offering will be used to fund private lending operations to qualified borrowers generating contractual cash flows. The fund deploys capital on a direct, bilateral basis.
Fees
The fund charges zero management fees, zero performance fees, and zero carried interest. The GP earns through the spread between borrower payments and investor distributions.
Regulatory Status
Corinthian Capital Fund LLC relies on the Section 3(c)(1) exemption from registration under the Investment Company Act of 1940. Securities have not been and will not be registered under the Securities Act of 1933 or any state securities laws.
