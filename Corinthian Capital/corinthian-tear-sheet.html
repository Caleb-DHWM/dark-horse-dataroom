The Fund
Corinthian Capital Fund LLC is an operational private debt fund offering a fixed 2% monthly return (24% annually) through private lending. Zero management fees. Zero performance fees. Reg D 506(b). Pre-existing relationship required.
Strategy
Private lending to cash-flowing e-commerce businesses generating fixed contractual returns. Direct, bilateral lending with full operational visibility.
Key Differentiators
Zero fee structure eliminates fee drag entirely. Fixed contractual returns paid monthly. Operational track record as proof-of-concept for the broader Dark Horse fund family. GP alignment through principal capital commitment.
