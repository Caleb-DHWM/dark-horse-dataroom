The Fund

Corinthian Capital Fund LLC

Texas LLC · Reg D 506(b) · Section 3(c)(1)
Corinthian Capital Partners is an established private debt fund offering investors a fixed contractual return of 2% per month (24% per annum) through senior secured lending to a single institutional borrower. The fund charges no management fees and no performance fees. Structured under Reg D 506(b), participation requires a pre-existing substantive relationship with the issuer. Jointly managed by Dark Horse Wealth Management LLC and Atlas Wealth Management.

Corinthian Capital Partners LLC
Corinthian Capital Partners LLC is a Texas limited liability company jointly managed by Dark Horse Wealth Management LLC and Atlas Wealth Management. Securities are offered pursuant to Regulation D, Rule 506(b) under the Securities Act of 1933. No general solicitation or advertising is permitted. All investors must have a pre-existing substantive relationship with the issuer. The fund relies on Section 3(c)(1) exemption and is limited to no more than 100 beneficial owners. The stated 2% monthly / 24% annual return is a contractual target rate and is not guaranteed. All investments involve risk including possible loss of principal. Securities have not been and will not be registered under the Securities Act of 1933.
