Monday ToG Wk1: "In the Beginning" — Map Intro & Creation Timeline

Clive

Morning Meeting
Bible: Genesis 1:1–5, Pledge of Allegiance + Christian flag pledge, discuss "what does 'ancient' mean?"
Math Mammoth Grade 3 — Ch. 1
Review place value through thousands; lesson "Thousands" p.10–11. Complete 10 problems independently.
ToG — Map Work
Label blank world map: Fertile Crescent, Tigris & Euphrates rivers, Mesopotamia. Color land/water.
ToG — Timeline Card
Place "Creation" card at 4000 BC on timeline. Read Story of the World Vol.1 pp.1–3 (intro). Write 2-sentence preview in history notebook.
Afternoon — AAS Level 3
Unit 1 Word List introduction; complete Step 1 spelling activity. HWT: Cursive practice p.1–2.

Gideon

Math Mammoth Grade 2 — Ch. 1
Review counting to 1,000; lesson "Counting in Hundreds" p.8–9. Use base-ten blocks if needed. 8 problems.
ToG — Map Work
Point to and say: Tigris, Euphrates, Fertile Crescent. Color map with Clive — narrate 2 things he notices.
ToG — Timeline Card
Place "Creation" card. Listen to Ch.1 intro (Kyler reads aloud). Oral narration: "What did God make?"
🔴 AAR — Lesson 1 Review (20-30 min one-on-one)
Review phonogram cards: short vowels a, e, i, o, u. Blend 3-letter CVC words (cat, bed, sit, hot, bug). Read 5 decodable words from flashcard deck. Practice segmenting 1 word orally.
Afternoon — HWT + Reading Practice
HWT: Lowercase b, d, p (reversals focus). Reading practice: re-read decodable reader from AAR deck.

Simon

Morning Meeting
Say pledge with brothers; clap & count to 10; sing calendar song.
Dimensions Math K
Chapter 1: Matching & Sorting. Sort 10 objects by color. "Which are the same?"
ToG — Picture Books
Read: In the Beginning (picture Bible). Point to the world map; say "earth" and "ocean." Color page.
Activity Bin (11:15–11:45)
Playdough, puzzles, or lacing cards while Kyler does Gideon's AAR.
Tuesday SotW Vol.1 Ch.1 Read-Aloud — The Earliest People

Clive

Math Mammoth Gr3 — Ch.1 cont.

AAS Level 3 (independent)
Unit 1 Step 2: practice word sort. Sort words into short-vowel groups.

Draw a picture of "people who lived long ago." Dictate 1–2 sentences to Kyler to write beneath.

Afternoon — HWT + Reading
HWT: Lowercase l, k, f (tall letters). Re-read today's decodable sentences aloud 2 times.

Pattern blocks, stacking cups, or shape sorter during Gideon's AAR block.
