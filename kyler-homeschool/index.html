📖 Bible/Christian Studies — Not on the main schedule?

Memoria Press Christian Studies is designed to be woven into the ToG Core block, not a separate time slot. Each boy uses his level during the 8:30–9:30 block: Clive = Year 3 (NT/Gospels), Gideon = Year 2 (OT/Kings & Prophets), Simon = picture/story level. 🧡 Simon's Activity Rotation

Simon (age 4) needs structured independent activities during Gideon's OG literacy block (10:15–10:45) and any other windows where Kyler is focused on the older boys. The key: prep the bins the night before, rotate daily so they stay fresh, limit to 3–4 items max per bin.

Each "bin" = a small tray, basket, or plastic container. Set it out silently while Simon is on break — he discovers it when he returns. This prevents the "what do I do?" loop.
