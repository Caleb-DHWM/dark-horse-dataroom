Dark Horse Wealth Management — Platform Overview

Dark Horse Wealth Management is a private alternative asset management platform purpose-built to deliver institutional-grade private credit and commodity trade finance across three distinct, complementary fund mandates. Each fund is independently structured, collateral-backed, and zero-fee—combining the precision of a boutique manager with the infrastructure of an institutional-scale operation. The platform serves a spectrum of investors from verified accredited individuals to sovereign wealth funds and qualified purchasers, deploying capital across U.S. structured credit, global hard assets, and physical precious metals trade finance. Dark Horse does not compete with its funds; it architects a system where investors find their optimal entry point and scale naturally within the family as their allocation grows.

Platform Architecture

Why Two Funds

Each mandate is independent — and together they form a complete institutional private credit ecosystem.

Complementary by Design

The Dark Horse fund family was not built as competing products — it was architected as complementary instruments occupying distinct positions on the risk and diversification spectrum. Draft Horse Capital Partners delivers depth: a single high-conviction credit relationship with five layers of collateral, maximum structural precision, and full investor transparency into one counterparty — ideal for investors who want to know exactly what backs their yield. Quarter Horse Capital Partners delivers breadth: nine uncorrelated hard-asset classes in a single vehicle, eliminating the need for an investor to allocate across multiple managers while maintaining institutional-grade private credit yields — ideal for those who prefer diversified exposure with one allocation decision. Together, these funds allow the Dark Horse platform to serve the full institutional capital stack — from a $200,000 accredited investor entry point — with zero management fees at every level.

Draft Horse — Depth & Precision
Single counterparty. Five collateral layers. Full structural transparency. For investors who want to own one thing and know everything about it.
Quarter Horse — Breadth & Diversification
Nine uncorrelated asset classes. One allocation. No manager-selection burden. For investors who want institutional yield across hard assets without the complexity of multi-manager portfolios.
