Physical Collateral Enforcement Pathway
Step 1
Default Event
Borrower failure to repay or covenant breach triggers enforcement rights
Step 2
LBMA Vault Hold
Physical collateral placed on hold at Brinks/Transguard — no release without Fund authorization
Step 3
Legal Notice
Formal notice served; Texas governing law; secured creditor rights enforced per loan agreement
Step 4
Physical Seizure
Custodian transfers physical commodity to Fund control per collateral agreement
Step 5
Liquidation
LBMA commodity sold through confirmed buyers at spot; globally liquid, 24/7 priced
Physical gold and silver are among the most liquid assets in the world — LBMA Good Delivery bars trade 24 hours/day across global OTC markets with immediate pricing transparency. Collateral enforcement timeline is materially shorter than traditional private credit.
