Fund Structure

Investment Vehicle: Deal-by-deal private debt fund. Each deal presented independently with its own terms, duration, rate, and risk profile. No blind pool.

Regulation: 506(b) — pre-existing substantive relationship required. No general solicitation. Up to 35 sophisticated non-accredited investors alongside unlimited accredited investors.

Principal Alignment: Caleb Caraway, sole owner and Managing Member, co-invests personal capital alongside investors in every deal.

Deal Origination Pipeline

  • Private Credit: Direct lending to operating businesses with tangible asset backing
  • Art Acquisition Financing: Secured lending against authenticated fine art and collectibles
  • Opportunistic Deals: Time-sensitive opportunities across specialty markets
  • Platform Deployments: Alongside Draft Horse Capital Partners on select credit facilities

Deal-by-Deal Approach

Each deal is presented to qualified investors individually with full terms disclosed upfront. Investors choose which deals to participate in. No capital calls on uninvested commitments. Rate, duration, collateral description, and risk factors provided per deal memo.

Investor Advantages

Full Transparency

Every deal presented with complete terms, collateral description, and risk factors before commitment.

Zero Fees

No management fee, no performance fee, no carried interest. Fund economics from borrower-investor spread.

Principal Alignment

Managing Member invests alongside every deal. Same position, same risk, same terms.

Platform Integration

Dark Horse Wealth Management Platform

Quarter Horse sources deals from the broader Dark Horse platform's operating verticals — aviation, mining & metals, real estate, specialty lending, and art-backed securities. Each vertical has dedicated domain expertise and an independent deal pipeline, providing diversified origination for Quarter Horse investors.
