Regulatory Framework

Regulation D Rule 506(b)

Pre-Existing Substantive Relationship
All investors must have a substantive pre-existing relationship with the Managing Member. This is not a limitation — it is a feature that ensures every investor relationship is personal and direct.
No General Solicitation
This offering is not publicly advertised. It is available only through direct communication with the Managing Member and his team. This protects both investors and the fund.
Investor Composition
Up to 35 sophisticated non-accredited investors may participate, alongside unlimited accredited investors. Each investor receives full deal transparency and opportunity to evaluate on merit.
The 506(b) framework ensures that every investment relationship is substantive, personal, and built on direct dialogue and confidence — not impersonal capital allocation into blind pools.
