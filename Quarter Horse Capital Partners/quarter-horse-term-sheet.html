Anchor: Field Futures Texas — 670-acre silver ore body, Llano County, TX; 4,431 ppm confirmed by 3 independent labs (ALS ISO/IEC 17025, UT Austin, Rocky Mountain Assay). SPV also deploys into structured royalty agreements. QH holds security interest in mineral rights, surface rights, and resource body. 16% Mineral rights + surface rights + royalty assignments
4 Aviation: Jet Charter Company Credit Facility QH lends $200M to a FAA Part 135 certificated jet charter company (identity confidential; disclosed under NDA) for Gulfstream G550 acquisition. Charter company manages fleet, generates charter revenue, returns distributions to QH from NOI. Current fleet: 4 G4-class aircraft. Per-G550 NOI (100%): $2.1M–$3.1M/year. QH holds first-priority lien: UCC-1 + FAA Civil Aviation Registry (49 U.S.C. § 44107) per aircraft. 15% UCC-1 + FAA Civil Aviation Registry lien per aircraft
5 Energy & Technology Infrastructure SPV QH lends $100M to Energy & Technology Infrastructure SPVs across two convergent mandates: (A) clean energy transition — wind, solar, battery storage, SMR, grid modernization; and (B) data center & AI power infrastructure — mission-critical power, data center operators, AI computing facilities, edge computing. Both mandates share the same borrower universe and collateral type. Structures: private project finance (18–60 mo), production/capacity royalties, venture debt. QH holds security interest in project assets, offtake/capacity agreements, IP licenses, and government contracts. 20% Project assets + offtake/capacity agreements + government contracts
6 Fine Art SPV ($541M Collection) 20% Mirrors SPV assets: RE, aircraft, mineral rights, art, commodity, energy

Material Disclosures
Fine Art — JP Morgan Credit Facility Disclosure
The Fine Art allocation involves the use of a third-party institutional credit facility with JP Morgan, utilizing a curated $541M fine art collection (curated by Elena Kohn / Sotheby's) as collateral at loan-to-value ratios of 30–50% on blue-chip works (approximately 70% of the art allocation). The remaining 30% consists of "red-chip" works targeted for sale within 120 days. Credit facility terms, availability, and LTV ratios are subject to JP Morgan's ongoing credit determination and are not guaranteed. The use of leverage in this allocation amplifies both potential returns and potential losses.
No Early Redemption
All capital committed to Quarter Horse Capital Partners LLC is subject to a full 3-year lockup. There is absolutely NO mechanism for early redemption under any circumstances. 100% of principal is locked until the expiration of the 3-year term. Investors must be prepared to have their capital fully illiquid for the entire 3-year period. Investors should ensure this term is consistent with their liquidity requirements before subscribing. This is not a liquid investment.
