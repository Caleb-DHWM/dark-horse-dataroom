Risk Factors

Deal-Specific Risk
Each deal carries its own risk profile. Returns depend on the performance of the individual borrower and the specific collateral arrangement. Past deal performance does not predict future results.
Illiquidity Risk
Investor capital is locked for the duration of each deal. There is no secondary market for fund interests and no early redemption mechanism.
Concentration Risk
Individual deals may represent concentrated exposure to a single counterparty, sector, or asset class. Investors should consider portfolio-level diversification.
Regulatory Risk
The fund relies on Regulation D Rule 506(b) exemption. Changes in securities law or interpretive guidance could affect fund operations.
Platform Risk
Deal origination depends on the Dark Horse Wealth Management platform. Disruption to the platform could affect deal flow.
Key Person Risk
Caleb Caraway is the sole principal. The fund's operations depend entirely on his continued involvement and judgment.

This term sheet is provided for discussion purposes only and does not constitute an offer to sell or a solicitation to buy any securities. The information contained herein is confidential and proprietary and is intended solely for the review of prospective investors with a pre-existing substantive relationship with Caleb Caraway, the Managing Member of Quarter Horse Capital Partners LLC.

Quarter Horse Capital Partners LLC is offered in reliance upon the exemption from registration provided by Regulation D, Rule 506(b) of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the "Securities Act"). This exemption is available only to investors who have a pre-existing substantive relationship with the Managing Member and does not permit general solicitation or advertising. Prospective investors must be either (i) accredited investors as defined under Rule 501(a) of the Securities Act, or (ii) sophisticated investors meeting the requirements of Rule 506(b)(2)(ii). In no event may there be more than 35 non-accredited investors in the Fund.

The Fund is also exempt from registration as an investment company under §3(c)(1) of the Investment Company Act of 1940, which exemption is available only to issuers that do not have more than 100 beneficial owners of their outstanding securities and that do not make or propose to make a public offering of their securities.

Each prospective investor acknowledges that: (i) the securities offered are highly illiquid; (ii) there is no secondary market for fund interests; (iii) investor capital will be locked for the duration of each deal with no early withdrawal or redemption rights; (iv) each deal presents its own distinct risk profile; (v) concentration and platform risks exist; (vi) the fund depends upon the continued participation and judgment of Caleb Caraway; and (vii) all material conflicts of interest will be disclosed in deal-specific offering materials.

No offer or sale of fund interests may proceed without the execution of final offering documents prepared by SCALE LLP, including a deal-specific limited liability company agreement, subscription agreement, and private placement memorandum containing complete risk factors, use of proceeds, and other material information. This term sheet does not replace or supersede those final documents, which will govern the rights and obligations of all parties.

Past performance is not indicative of future results. All investors are advised to consult with their own legal, tax, and financial advisors before making any investment decision. Prospective investors should conduct their own independent due diligence and risk assessment with respect to each deal opportunity presented.
