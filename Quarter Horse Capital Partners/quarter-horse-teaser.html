Pre-Existing Relationship Requirement
Reg D 506(b) Compliance
Quarter Horse operates under Regulation D Rule 506(b), which permits capital raising from investors with whom the fund manager has a pre-existing substantive relationship. There is no general solicitation and no public advertising. This requirement ensures that every investor relationship is built on direct, personal engagement with the principal — not mass marketing. Up to 35 sophisticated non-accredited investors may participate alongside unlimited accredited investors.
